Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how E-Hospitality collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details like name, email, age, and contact number during registration.
  • Medical information shared for appointment and prescription purposes.
  • Login data such as IP address, device type, and browser information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Facilitate doctor-patient interactions
  • Provide medical appointment and prescription services
  • Improve our platform experience

3. Data Protection

We implement strong security measures to protect your data. Only authorized personnel (like your doctor) can access your medical records.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties unless legally required or with your explicit consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Ask us to update or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent for data usage at any time

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at support@ehospitality.com.
