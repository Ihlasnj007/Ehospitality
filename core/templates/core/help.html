Help Center

For Patients

How do I book an appointment?

Register or log in as a patient, then go to the Appointments section to choose a doctor and time slot.

Where can I see my prescriptions?

After a doctor updates your appointment, you can view prescriptions under the "Prescriptions" tab in your dashboard.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Please contact support at support@ehospitality.com for assistance.

For Doctors

How do I view my appointments?

Log in as a doctor and go to the "My Appointments" section to see your schedule.

How do I prescribe medicines?

Click on a patient's appointment in your dashboard and fill in the prescription details.

How do I manage patient records?

You can only view and prescribe for patients who have booked appointments with you.
