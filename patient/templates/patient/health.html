Weekly Health Tips

Heart Health

  • Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week
  • Limit saturated fats and opt for healthy oils like olive oil
  • Monitor your blood pressure regularly after age 40
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques like deep breathing

Nutrition Tips

  • Fill half your plate with colorful fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined carbohydrates
  • Stay hydrated with water instead of sugary drinks
  • Practice mindful eating by slowing down during meals

Mental Wellness

  • Prioritize 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night
  • Take regular breaks from screens throughout the day
  • Maintain social connections with friends and family
  • Practice gratitude by noting 3 positive things daily
